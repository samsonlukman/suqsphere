Permanent Account Deletion

We understand that you wish to terminate your account. Please read the following before proceeding.

What You Are Losing

  • Content Deletion: All your posts, photos, likes, and direct messages will be **permanently and irrevocably deleted**.
  • Market History: All your active seller listings will be removed. You will lose access to your sales and purchase history.
  • Data Loss: This process is final and cannot be reversed.

Procedure for Account Deletion

To ensure the request is secure, please send an email verification from the address associated with your account.

Step 1: Send Verification Email

Send an email from the address associated with your Suqsphere account to:

support@suqsphere.com

Step 2: Use the Required Subject Line

The subject line **MUST** be:

ACCOUNT DELETION REQUEST - [Your Username]

Your account will be deactivated upon verified receipt and fully deleted within 30 days.
