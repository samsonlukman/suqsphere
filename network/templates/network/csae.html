Suqsphere Child Safety & Reporting Policy

Suqsphere is committed to maintaining a safe, ethical, and supportive environment for all users, with zero tolerance for content related to Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

1. Zero Tolerance for CSAE

Any content or behavior related to Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, the creation, distribution, display, or access of any material that exploits children. Violations will result in:

  • Immediate and permanent suspension of the userâ€™s account.
  • Mandatory reporting of the user and the content to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement agencies.

2. User Safety Standards

2.1 Age Restriction

The Suqsphere Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We reserve the right to terminate any account found to be owned by a user under this age limit.

2.2 Reporting of Illegal Content

Users must use the in-app reporting tool (accessible via user profiles or post menus) or the external contact methods provided below to report any content that:

  • Depicts or suggests the abuse or exploitation of children.
  • Involves child endangerment, human trafficking, or other illegal activity.

3. Contact Information for Reporting Violations

For urgent reports related to Child Safety, please use the following contact information. We commit to reviewing and responding to all safety reports within **24 hours**.

Primary Contact Method:

Email: safety@suqsphere.com

In-App Reporting: Flag button on posts/comments or Report User function on profiles.
